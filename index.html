Fairs & Festivals

The life of Gujarat

Rann Utsav

Kutch or Rannutsav, a unique manifestation of the varied cultural ethos of the region is known for its ethnic flavor and celebrative zest.

Kite Festival

The festival of Uttarayan is a uniquely Gujarati phenomenon, when the skies over most cities of the state fill with kites from before dawn until well after dark.

Tarnetar fair

Based on the legend of Draupadi's swayamvara, this fair is a celebration of ethnic Gujarat’s folk-dance, music, costumes and the arts
